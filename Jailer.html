Jailer

Muthuvel Pandian is a retired jailer living happily with his wife, son, daughter-in-law and grandson. His son who is an Assistant Commissioner of police goes missing while investigating the case of missing temple idols and is presumed dead. The story is about how Muthuvel Pandian goes toe to toe against Varma, the mastermind behind large-scale smuggling of temple idols and treasures, and avenges his son.
